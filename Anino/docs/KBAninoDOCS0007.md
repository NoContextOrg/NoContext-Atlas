## 🎯 Asset Categories & Why They Matter

### Environmental Assets
- **Wide route shots** – Establish locations, game world scope
- **Ground textures** – Dirt, gravel surfaces for terrain materials
- **Close-up details** – Grass, rocks, wear patterns for realism
- **Sky/lighting** – Time-of-day variations, atmospheric effects
- **Neutral shots** – Backgrounds for menus, loading screens

### Motion & Animation
- **Crowd footage** – Movement patterns, spacing, behavior
- **Marching sequences** – Walking cycles, group dynamics
- **Panning shots** – Environmental exploration, camera movement reference
- **Walking clips** – Animation keyframes, stride length

### Audio Elements
- **Crowd ambience** – Emotional tone, density variations
- **Footsteps** – Surface-specific sounds (dirt vs pavement)
- **Clean ambient** – Pure environment without people
- **Transitions** – Dynamic audio changes (silence to crowd)

---

## 🎬 Capture Best Practices

### Steady Recording
- Use tripod/gimbal for 30–60 sec sequences
- Keep hands steady if handheld (brace against body)
- Avoid panning unless intentional

### Lighting Awareness
- Morning (6–9 AM) – Soft, long shadows, golden hour
- Noon (11 AM–1 PM) – Harsh shadows, high contrast
- Afternoon (2–5 PM) – Warm tones, moderate shadows
- Capture all times for variety

### Angle Variation
- Eye level – Normal perspective
- Low angle – Ground/texture focus
- High angle – Group/formation overview
- Wide shots – Environment context

---

## 🔍 Real-Time Quality Checks

### Video Quality Indicators
- **Good:** Sharp focus, minimal shake, clear audio
- **Retake:** Blurry, motion blur, audio clipping, sudden noise
- **Corrupt:** Freezes, skipped frames, audio sync issues

### Image Quality Indicators
- **Good:** Sharp details, proper exposure, natural colors
- **Retake:** Blurry, overexposed (blown highlights), underexposed (dark shadows)
- **Unusable:** Motion blur, accidental fingers in frame, completely out of focus

### Audio Quality Indicators
- **Good:** Clear, audible, no background noise, proper levels
- **Retake:** Clipping (distorted peaks), wind noise, sudden interruptions
- **Unusable:** Inaudible, severely distorted, corrupted file

## ⚙️ Decision Tree: "Is This Usable?"

```
START: Have you captured this asset?
  |
  └─→ Is it technically sharp/clear?
      ├─ NO → RETAKE (bad quality, not usable)
      └─ YES → Is it game-relevant?
          ├─ NO → SKIP (interesting but not useful)
          └─ YES → Do we already have something similar?
              ├─ YES (exact duplicate) → SKIP
              ├─ SIMILAR (but different angle/time) → KEEP
              └─ NO (unique) → KEEP AS PRIORITY
```

## 🔐 Security Protocols

### Device Security
- PIN/password protection on all devices
- Don't leave devices unattended in crowded areas
- Keep backup drives in waterproof, secure case
- Separate primary device and backup drives (different people)

### Data Integrity
- Never delete originals before backup verified
- Don't edit files on primary device (work on copies)
- Keep metadata (field notes) with file collections
- Generate checksums (sha256sum) for final archive

### Environmental Protection
- Waterproof containers for drives
- Keep devices in padded bag/case
- Protect from extreme heat/cold
- Monitor humidity in storage areas
